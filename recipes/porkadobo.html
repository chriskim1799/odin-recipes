Pork Adobo Recipe

Description

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added. Adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines, along with Sinigang.

Ingredients

Base

  • 2lbs pork belly
  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 4 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp peppercorn
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic. Let marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put in the marinated pork belly, then cook for ~4-5 minutes.
  3. Pour remaining marinade into pot including garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves then bring to boil. Simmer for ~40 minutes to an hour.
  5. Put in the vinegar and simmer for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste, and serve hot. Congrats, you have pork adobo for dinner!
